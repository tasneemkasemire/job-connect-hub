Frequently Asked Questions

This page covers common questions about using the platform.

How do I apply for a job?

Create an account, browse listings, open a job card, and click the apply button to submit your details.

Are the job listings verified?

Yes. The platform is designed to prioritize reliable and verified opportunities to reduce fake job information.

Can employers post jobs on the platform?

Yes. Employers can post roles through the admin and employer management process for approval and publishing.

Is the website mobile friendly?

Yes. The layout is responsive so users can browse opportunities on phones, tablets, and desktop devices.

Can I save jobs for later?

Yes. The user dashboard supports saved jobs and application status tracking.
